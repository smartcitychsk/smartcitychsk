## About Us

Jiangsu Chonghua Digital Technology Co., Ltd. (**CHSK**) is an integrated smart city service provider affiliated with Chongqing University Liyang Smart City Research Institute.

Guided by the mission of **creating sustainable value for the engineering industry through digital technology**, CHSK is powered by the core capabilities of the **Citycore Digital Engine**. We provide solutions for intelligent construction, digital archives, digital cities, and full-process BIM consulting. We serve governments, industrial parks, transportation infrastructure, water utilities, construction projects, and urban governance scenarios, helping customers accelerate digital transformation and build smarter urban infrastructure.

## Business Areas

### 🏗 Intelligent Construction

We provide full-lifecycle digital capabilities for engineering projects, including digital applications, design management, schedule management, quality management, material management, equipment management, and safety management. These capabilities help projects achieve collaborative, refined, and data-driven management.

### 🚰 Smart Water

For water supply and drainage, water environment, and watershed management scenarios, CHSK builds capabilities such as smart sensing across full water systems, integrated applications, mobile applications, joint dispatching, statistical analysis, permanent digital archives, and 3D visualized “one-map” management.

### 🏙 Digital Cities

CHSK serves digital city scenarios including smart parks, smart transportation, smart parking, emergency response, intelligent security, public services, smart education, and smart healthcare. We help build city-level data foundations and visual governance platforms.

### 📁 Digital Archives

For engineering projects, transportation construction, and urban infrastructure, CHSK supports electronic record formation, e-signatures/e-seals, signing audit trails, signature verification, tamper resistance, archiving, retrieval, utilization, and secure management, enabling trusted, standardized, and closed-loop digital archive management.

## Research & Innovation

Chongqing University Liyang Smart City Research Institute focuses on talent cultivation, technological innovation, and industrial aggregation. Its research directions include intelligent construction, intelligent design and inspection, smart underground space, smart environmental protection, smart energy, smart transportation, modern physics, and smart urban-rural planning and design.

CHSK continues to invest in:

## Business Capabilities & Technical Modules

Focusing on digital archives, intelligent construction, and digital cities, CHSK has built integrated technical capabilities from data collection, business modeling, and platform development to visualized applications.

- **Digital Archives Modules**: electronic record collection, e-signatures/e-seals, signing audit trails, signature verification, tamper resistance, archive classification and filing, metadata management, full-text search, permission control, long-term preservation, archive utilization, and data governance.
- **Intelligent Construction Modules**: project collaboration, schedule management, quality and safety management, material and equipment management, BIM applications, smart construction sites, and AI data cockpits.
- **Digital City Modules**: CIM foundation platforms, GIS spatial services, digital twin scenarios, city operation monitoring, smart parks, smart transportation, and smart water.
- **Data Foundation Capabilities**: multi-source data integration, data cleansing and governance, master data management, indicator systems, visualization dashboards, data analytics, and decision support.
- **Platform Engineering Capabilities**: microservices architecture, cloud-native deployment, unified identity authentication, permission systems, low-code configuration, system integration, and open APIs.
